Welcome to Phonk Zone

Phonk is a subgenre of hip hop and trap that grew out of the underground Memphis rap scene of the 1990s. It blends old-school grit with modern energy, creating music that feels raw, dark, and atmospheric.

  • Memphis Roots: raw and gritty inspiration from the 90s.
  • Chopped & Screwed: slowed and distorted for that eerie touch.
  • Lo-fi & Ghostly: hypnotic and atmospheric soundscapes.
  • Modern Twist: heavy 808s, cowbells, and trap energy.
  • Drift Phonk: bass-driven beats tied to street racing culture.
